Schedule a P2P AllReduce Sum operation between current rank and rank_mate.

A P2P AllReduce Sum operation, i.e. an all-reduce involving just two ranks with MPI_SUM as op, is performed between the rank where this function is called and rank_mate.

in is a sender of a read-only tile that, together with the one received from rank_mate, will be summed in out (on both ranks)
