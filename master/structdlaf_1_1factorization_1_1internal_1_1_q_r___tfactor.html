Forms the triangular factor T of a block of reflectors H, which is defined as a product of k elementary reflectors.

It is similar to what xLARFT in LAPACK does. Given k elementary reflectors stored in the column of v starting at tile v_start, together with related tau values in taus, in t will be formed the triangular factor for the H block of reflector, such that

H = I - V . T . V*

where H = H1 . H2 . ... . Hk

in which Hi represents a single elementary reflector transformation

Parameters
kthe number of elementary reflectors to use (from the beginning of the tile)
vwhere the elementary reflectors are stored
v_starttile in v where the column of reflectors starts
taustile of the taus vector, associated with the related elementary reflector
ttile where the resulting T factor will be stored in its top-left sub-matrix of size TileElementSize(k, k)
mpi_col_task_chainwhere internal communications are issued
Precondition
k <= t.get().size().rows && k <= t.get().size().cols()
k >= 0
v_start.isIn(v.nrTiles())
